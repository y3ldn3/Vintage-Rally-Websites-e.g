About Vintage Rally Club

What Is Vintage Rally Club About

In the Sports Car Club of the World, VintageRally is the most popular and easily accessible extreme dirt motorsport, making it the ideal venue for determining whether you have what it takes to powerslide your way to victory. Imagine a scaled-down rally stage being set up on a patch of unpaved ground, with traffic cones used to mark the route instead of trees or rocks. In most regions, all that is required is to show up at event registration with a sound, hardtop vehicle and the entry fee because the entry fees and equipment requirements are significantly lower than those needed to enter any other kind of performance rally. Many SCCA Regions loan out helmets and will help a new competitor with registration.

Our Mission and Goals

Our goal is to create a country that is motivated by our shared love of motorsports, past, present, and future. a game that benefits from our unrivaled knowledge of motorsport technology, our spirit of competition, and our strong ties to the local community. Our goal is to encourage and facilitate increased participation in our sport in a setting that is fair, inclusive, entertaining, and progressive. should utilize our power and influence to secure the future of our sport.

Our Commitment to Equality, Diversity and Inclusion in Motorsport

We are honored to serve the interests of motorsport in the UK, a country inspired by the past, present, and future of a sport that benefits from our strong sense of community, technological prowess that is unmatched in the world, and competitive spirit. Our goal is to promote motorsport involvement and make it possible in a progressive, safe, fun, and fair environment. Everyone should have the chance to reach their full potential, from the underprivileged to the top professionals. In addition to fulfilling our fundamental responsibilities, we are dedicated to successfully utilizing our influence and reach to benefit society as a whole.
