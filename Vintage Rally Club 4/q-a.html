Recently Asked Questions

The following are the most recurring questions

Is V-R-C international?

Vintage Rally Club is a small club that can be found globally. At the moment we are not 100% international but we have rally races under the name vintage rally club in every single continent on the face of this earth.


Has There been any accidents?

As every rally on the face of this earth should be doing is regarding the saftey of both drivers and bystanders. We have precaustions set just in case a crash ever does happen but before that we have precaustions so that a crash will never happen. If any accident happens in vintage rally club then the expenses will be handled by the rally club. For both drivers and fans who wanted to watch....Safety is our first priority...


Why Do You Use Vintage Cars?

Well... the reason why we use vintage cars is because we based our whole rally on Vintage rally club. The idea came about when the founder went through the garage and found alot of cars that were vintage and at cheap prices. Since he was a veteran of rallys he felt hurt to see such good horsepower go to waste. So he dedicated his idea to honor all the vintage cars and all the heros that drove them. Welcome to "VINTAGE" Rally Club lol.
