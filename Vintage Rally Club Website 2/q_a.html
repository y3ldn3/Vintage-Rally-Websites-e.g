2. How fast can rally cars go?

It's not about complete high speed with rally cars; it's about the speed the car can create when driving around turns. Rally stages are closed public roads, therefore they rarely include longer straights like drag racing.

4. Why do rally cars backfire and how?

The anti-lag system, which squirts gasoline directly into the exhaust manifold before the turbo to maintain the turbo spinning at a high speed so there is no turbo lag after the green light turns on, causes the backfire and loud popping.
